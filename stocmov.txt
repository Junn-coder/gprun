Q: I want to learn from you on how to buy and sell usa stock. Say, buy one good and sell high. Give me some principles on tech shares buying and selling.
A: When investing in US tech stocks, "buy low and sell high" is the fundamental goal, but the tech sector requires
specific principles due to its high volatility, rapid innovation cycles, and growth-oriented valuations.

Here are core principles for buying and selling US tech stocks:

### 1. Understand the Business, Not Just the Ticker
*   **The Principle:** Never buy a tech company just because the stock price is rising. Understand what they build, how
they make money (software subscriptions, hardware sales, advertising), and their "moat" (competitive advantage).
*   **Action:** Before buying, ask: "Will this company's product be essential 5 years from now?" (e.g., cloud computing,
AI infrastructure).

### 2. Differentiate Between "Growth" and "Value" in Tech
*   **The Principle:** Some tech companies are mature cash-cows (like Apple or Microsoft), while others are hyper-growth
companies burning cash to gain market share.
*   **Action:** Mature tech provides stability and buybacks/dividends. High-growth tech provides massive upside but
carries the risk of heavy crashes if interest rates rise or growth slows. Balance both in your portfolio.

### 3. The Power of "Dollar-Cost Averaging" (DCA)
*   **The Principle:** Timing the tech market perfectly is impossible. Tech stocks can drop 20% in a week on bad news
and recover a month later.
*   **Action:** Instead of buying all at once, invest a set amount of money at regular intervals (e.g., buying every
month). This ensures you buy more shares when prices are low and fewer when they are high.

### 4. Watch the Macro Environment (Interest Rates)
*   **The Principle:** Tech stock valuations are heavily tied to interest rates. When the Federal Reserve raises interest
rates, borrowing money becomes expensive, and the future profits of growth tech companies are discounted, causing stock
prices to fall.
*   **Action:** Be cautious about buying heavily unprofitable tech stocks during periods of rapidly rising interest rates.

### 5. Selling Principles: Have a Plan Before You Buy
*   **The Principle:** Don't let emotions dictate when you sell. Fear causes selling at the bottom; greed causes holding
too long at the top.
*   **Action:**
    *   **Take Profits:** If a stock goes on a massive run, consider selling a small portion (10-20%) to lock in gains.
    *   **Cut Losers based on fundamentals:** Sell if the *reason* you bought the stock has fundamentally changed (e.g.,
    losing market share to a competitor). Do not sell just because the broader market is down.

### 6. Diversify Within Tech
*   **The Principle:** The tech sector is broad. It includes semiconductors, SaaS (Software as a Service), cybersecurity,
consumer electronics, and AI.
*   **Action:** Don't put all your money into one niche. Spread your bets across different sub-sectors to minimize risk.

Topic: How to pick a hot A-share short-term leader (龙头) — filter methodology
Summary: Six-step filter for selecting a hot A-share 短线 龙头, plus entry trigger and hard rejects.
Theme strength comes first, then leader identification, technical setup, tape resistance, risk-reward gate, and
rejection list. Adapted from O'Neil/Minervini frameworks to A-share 题材 trading rhythm.
Key points:
- Theme first, stock second: a real 题材 needs >=3 涨停 in the 板块, positive 主力 净流入, and a clear
  政策/事件 catalyst. No theme strength means skip.
- Identify the 龙头, not 跟风: highest 连板 count, biggest 封单 金额, earliest 涨停 时间, and known 游资
  席位 on 龙虎榜. Buying 跟风 = providing exit liquidity to 龙头 holders.
- Technical setup: price > 20MA with 20MA rising; 流通市值 30-150亿 sweet spot; no overhead 套牢盘 (prefer
  历史新高 or 平台 breakout); 量价 配合 — 启动日 放量, 加速日 缩量 涨停 (一字/T字 = strongest).
- Tape resistance: on 大盘 跌 days, does it hold green or 涨停? Strength against the tape signals 主力 锁仓.
- Risk-reward gate: enter only if stop loss <= 4-5% (break of 启动日 low) and next target >= 10-15% (next
  涨停 or 题材 高度新高). Otherwise pass.
- Hard rejects: ST 股, 高位 4板以上 接力, 业绩雷 风险窗口 (财报前1周), 大股东 减持 公告, 解禁 临近.
- Trigger: 试仓 on 涨停 回封 or 次日 低开 不破 启动日 low. Add only on 新高 + 放量. Cut on day-2
  close red with 资金 净流出.
